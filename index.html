kito is a young Brazilian artist who turns feeling into music. After starting out in trap and surpassing 3 million streams, collaborating with names in the scene such as Derek, Jackdi, and Lil Drope, he shows that his identity goes beyond any label. His track Business with Derek has already passed HALF A MILLION streams and keeps growing strongly since release.

His first career album, Late Hours, is born as a versatility manifesto: an artistic shift blending atmospheric R&B, eighties synthpop, trap, and contemporary soul — all in English. Between lighter tracks like Halfway Gone, introspective moments, and striking melodies like Confessions, kito builds a signature aesthetic — emotional, modern, and introspective.

More than following a formula, kito seeks to create a universe where vulnerability, ambition, and lifestyle meet. Today, without worrying about labels or other people’s negativity, he makes music as his life moment demands, pouring his whole heart into every song. FROM BRAZIL TO THE WORLD!
