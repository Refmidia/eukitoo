kito (@eukitoo) is a 21-year-old singer from São Paulo, Brazil. Music has been part of his life since childhood — from drums to piano. In June 2023 he released his debut single Projeto Passado, building an audience that also knows him as “Boy do trap” and “Conde do trap.” With over 2 million streams, his lyrics reflect real stories, obstacles, and dreams, with strong charisma on social media and releases.

Albums such as Late Hours (2025) and Confessions Missing Tracks (2026) expand his sound across trap, R&B, and cinematic atmospheres. Verified on Instagram; partnerships via DM.

kito (@eukitoo) é cantor e artista de São Paulo, com 21 anos. Desde criança a música faz parte da sua vida — da bateria ao piano. Com mais de 2 milhões de streams, suas letras trazem histórias reais, obstáculos e sonhos, com carisma forte nas redes e nos lançamentos.
